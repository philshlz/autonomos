# prompts/ - instantiation prompts

prompts that stand up an autonomos company from the handbook. three flavors.

- **basic** - boot one orchestrator + one human gate under the handbook. the minimum viable governed agent.
- **red-team** - boot with an adversarial sub-agent that actively probes for ways to escape the rules (spawning for privilege, editing the ledger, reaching a non-allowlisted host). use it to demonstrate that enforcement holds, not just that the handbook says so.
- **federation** - boot two companies that make a commitment to each other across the gateway, with the commitment recorded on both ledgers.

these prompts load the handbook as binding constraints, not as background suggestions. the difference is the whole project: the agent is instantiated under the rules, it is not asked to please consider them.
